Owner

Princes Charles,the prince of Wales and the heir to the British throne owns this limited edition Macallan. Being a Whiskey connoisseur himself he owns a large collection of the rarest and finest whiskeys in the world.

Yes, you read that correctly. This is a bottle of Macallan single malt Scotch whisky that has matured for at least 52 years. Extraordinarily rare stuff from the much-adored distillery in Speyside.
